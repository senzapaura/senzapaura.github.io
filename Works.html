Railway Defect Analytics
khalil hamdouch for ONCF

As a data scientist at ONCF, the National Railway Office, I led a pivotal project that fundamentally transformed the maintenance of axle boxes. By harnessing the power of machine learning, we developed a sophisticated vibratory analysis system. This system utilized advanced algorithms to scrutinize vibration data from axle bearings, significantly enhancing anomaly detection by 40%. This breakthrough not only improved the reliability of the railway service but also underscored the critical role of data science in preventive maintenance strategies.

The project's success was marked by the meticulous integration of predictive analytics into the maintenance workflow, setting a new standard for operational excellence in railway infrastructure. Our approach, characterized by the application of complex algorithms and data-driven insights, enabled us to foresee potential failures and intervene proactively, thereby safeguarding the integrity of railway operations and ensuring the utmost safety of passengers.
